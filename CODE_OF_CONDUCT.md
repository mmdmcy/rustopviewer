# Code of Conduct

This project adopts the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/) Code of Conduct.

## Our Pledge

We as contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in the rustopviewer community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socioeconomic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Showing empathy and respect toward other people.
- Giving and receiving constructive feedback gracefully.
- Assuming good intent while still being honest about technical risks.
- Focusing discussion on what best serves users and the project.
- Helping newcomers get oriented and unblocked.

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, intimidation, or discriminatory language.
- Trolling, personal attacks, or inflammatory comments.
- Publishing private information without explicit permission.
- Repeatedly derailing discussions or refusing to engage in good faith.
- Any conduct that would reasonably make participation feel unsafe.

## Enforcement Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing standards of acceptable behavior and may take any action they deem appropriate in response to unacceptable behavior.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces, including issues, pull requests, discussions, release notes, and any other public or private communication channels used for the project.

## Reporting

To report a violation, contact the maintainers through a private GitHub security advisory or a direct maintainer channel when one is available. Reports will be reviewed promptly and handled as confidentially as possible.
